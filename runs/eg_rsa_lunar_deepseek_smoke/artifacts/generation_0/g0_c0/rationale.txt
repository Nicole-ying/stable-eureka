This reward function implements all required components from the schema. It uses dense shaping to guide the lander toward the pad: distance penalty encourages approach, velocity penalties encourage gentle descent, angle penalty encourages upright orientation. Progress rewards improvements in distance and velocity reduction. Terminal shaping gives +100 for successful landing (both legs contact, near pad, low speeds, upright) and large negatives for crashes/out-of-bounds. Landing bonus provides additional signal when conditions are met. Fuel and time penalties encourage efficiency. All components are bounded and use only public observation/action/info signals.
