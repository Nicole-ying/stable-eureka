Based on the feedback from generation 0, the main issues were: (1) shaping reward too weak relative to velocity penalty, (2) terminal bonus too small, (3) velocity penalty too harsh, (4) fuel penalty discourages necessary engine use. The new spec increases shaping magnitude (15x), reduces velocity penalty (-0.1), removes fuel penalty, and increases terminal bonus to 200. Terminal conditions are relaxed (position tolerance 0.2, velocity tolerance 0.5, angle tolerance 0.3) to make success more reachable.
