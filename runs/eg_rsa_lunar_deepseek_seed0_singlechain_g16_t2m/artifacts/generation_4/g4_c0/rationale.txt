The previous generation (g3_c0) failed catastrophically: the agent survived the full 1000 steps without landing because the per-step survival penalty (-0.1) was far too weak relative to shaping (~1.44 per step). The generated-private gap exploded to 1484.8 (52x private return). To fix this, we dramatically increase the survival penalty to -1.5 per step (making net per-step reward negative), reduce shaping to 1.0x, and increase terminal bonus to 1000. This targets: shaping ~50-100, velocity -20, angle -10, fuel -20, survival -1500, terminal 1000. The net per-step reward will be negative (~-1.5) when not landing, forcing the agent to seek the terminal bonus. This is a single major change (penalty magnitude) from g2_c0, which had private return 267.8.
