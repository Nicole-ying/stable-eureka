The previous generation (g2_c0) reduced shaping to 3x and added a terminal bonus of 500, achieving the first successful landing and a 58x improvement in private return (4.63 to 267.8). However, the generated-private gap remains large (534.5, ~2x private return). The key issue identified in feedback: the per-step survival penalty (-0.1) was planned but not implemented (fuel_efficiency was left at -0.0). This mutation adds a survival penalty of -0.1 per step via the fuel_efficiency component (renamed from zero to -0.1), reduces shaping multiplier from 3.0 to 2.0 to further reduce per-step accumulation, and slightly reduces velocity penalty from -0.5 to -0.4 to avoid overly penalizing necessary speed. These changes target a composition where terminal bonus dominates: survival penalty ~-29 (over ~290 steps), shaping ~180, velocity ~-30, angle ~-10, terminal 500. The terminal bonus remains at 500 with same tolerances (position 0.15, velocity 0.3, angle 0.2) since they were proven achievable.
