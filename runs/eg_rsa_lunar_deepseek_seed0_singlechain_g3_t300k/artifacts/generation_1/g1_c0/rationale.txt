This candidate addresses the key failure modes from the feedback:
1. **Nonlinear scaling**: Distance and velocity penalties use squared terms with higher coefficients (10x and 5x respectively) to create stronger gradients near the pad and discourage high speeds.
2. **Crash penalty**: Added explicit -100 penalty when `done=True` but landing conditions aren't met, strongly discouraging crashes.
3. **Engine usage**: Differentiated penalties for main engine (-0.5) vs side engines (-0.1) to encourage efficient use while still allowing maneuvering.
4. **Relaxed landing thresholds**: Slightly increased tolerances (0.15 vs 0.1) to make the terminal reward more achievable.
5. **Increased terminal reward**: Raised to 150 to better balance against the penalties.
6. **Single terminal component**: Combined landing bonus and crash penalty into one component to avoid double-counting terminal outcomes.
