This candidate addresses the critical failures from previous generations. It uses moderate linear penalties (-2*distance, -1*velocity) to avoid overwhelming the reward landscape. A progress shaping bonus rewards reductions in distance and velocity, providing a smooth gradient toward the pad and reducing the generated-private gap. The terminal reward is increased to +300 with relaxed thresholds (0.2), and the crash penalty is reduced to -50. Engine penalty is minimal (-0.1) to avoid discouraging necessary thrust. This balanced structure should guide the agent toward successful landings without freezing exploration.
