This reward function balances all required components to guide the lander toward a stable, upright landing at the pad. The landing bonus rewards perfect landings (both legs down, upright, low velocity) while penalizing bad landings. Distance, velocity, and angle penalties shape the agent toward the pad with controlled descent. Fuel penalty discourages unnecessary engine use. Crash penalty distinguishes between crashes and successful landings. Progress provides dense shaping for moving toward the pad. Stability penalizes spinning, effort penalizes inaction when far, and terminal provides bounded terminal shaping. All weights are tuned to keep total reward within [-1000, 1000].
